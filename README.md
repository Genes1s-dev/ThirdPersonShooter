# ThirdPersonShooter

Player controls:
<br>
WASD - movement  <br>
Shift - sprint <br>
Space - jump <br>
E - interract <br>
Mouse left click - fire <br>
Mouse right click - aim <br>
Tab - activate/switch weapon <br>

Task completed within ~ 25 hours, the lion's share of which was spent on animating these gorgeous legs
